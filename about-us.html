About Us

Since 1986, Drury Lane Sweets has been the provider of mouthwatering muffins, tarts, donuts and pastries. For decades we have provided award winning custom cakes and cupcakes that make your special occasions even more memorable. We use the highest quality ingredients and support local farmers when sourcing our eggs, seasonal berries and fruits. The results is the quality baked goods that you have come to know, love and depend on.

Something Sweeter

When you support Drury Lane Sweets you are part of something much bigger. Starting in 2004, our company has teamed with the Northeast Arc to sponsor local young adults with Autism in our internship program. With your support, we have been able to provide opportunities for students to come and learn valuable vocational skills in the kitchen and our cafe.
